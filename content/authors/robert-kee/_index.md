## Research Overview

Dr. Robert J. Kee is a Professor of Mechanical Engineering and holds the George R. Brown Distinguished Chair at the Colorado School of Mines. His research focuses on modeling and simulation of chemically reacting fluid flow for clean-energy applications, including fuel cells, advanced combustion, and electrochemical and materials-processing systems. His work connects elementary chemical and electrochemical kinetics with transport phenomena to advance next-generation energy technologies.

---

### Interests

- Chemically reacting flows  
- Electrochemical energy systems  
- Solid-oxide fuel cells  
- Catalytic combustion  
- Thin-film materials processing  
- Reaction–transport simulations
